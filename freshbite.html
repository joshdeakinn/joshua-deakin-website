Freshbite

Freshbite was a self-assigned freelance project focused on developing a brand identity for a company offering pre-prepared healthy meal boxes. The core concept was to communicate freshness, natural ingredients, and the idea that the food provides protection and nourishment for the body.

The visual direction draws on themes of growth and vitality, using natural colours and organic forms to reinforce the idea that the ingredients are fresh and responsibly sourced.

Through the identity and supporting materials, the goal was to create a brand that feels clean, trustworthy, and closely connected to nature — emphasising that what's inside the box is as fresh and beneficial as possible.

Midgard Greenhouse & Nursery

This project was commissioned during my freelance period by a client based in Canada who was looking to establish a visual identity for their business, Midgard Greenhouse and Nursery. The company grows fresh produce for local markets and sells a variety of shrubs and trees, but despite being established for several years, had never developed a formal corporate identity.

During our initial discussions, the founder shared his vision for the brand, which provided a strong starting point. My role was to translate that vision into a cohesive visual brand that would represent the business clearly and professionally.

Knowing the branding needed to work across staff uniforms, grocery bags, and a flag for the premises helped guide design decisions early — ensuring the final identity would remain clear, adaptable, and recognisable across different formats.

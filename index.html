Personal Project · Typography

Adrian Frutiger

This project was created during my time as a student at City of Glasgow College. Although it was some time ago now, it still holds a special place in my heart.

The concept was inspired by Adrian Frutiger's quote, "Typography must be as beautiful as a forest…"

I used the rings of a tree as a visual metaphor to represent the different stages of Frutiger's life and career — each ring symbolising a key chapter in his development as a designer.

Personal Project · Print Design

Rodger Casement

The work was based on the story of Roger Casement and the events surrounding his execution by hanging. I spent time researching the historical context to ensure the design choices reflected the period.

This included sourcing paper that closely resembled what would have been used during that time, helping the printed materials feel authentic to the era.

To represent Casement's Irish heritage, I worked with a palette of layered greens — deliberately allowing paint to bleed down the page, symbolising a smeared reputation.

Personal Project · Brand Identity

Freshbite

Freshbite was a self-assigned freelance project focused on developing a brand identity for a company offering pre-prepared healthy meal boxes.

The visual direction draws on themes of growth and vitality, using natural colours and organic forms to reinforce the idea that the ingredients are fresh and responsibly sourced.

The goal was to create a brand that feels clean, trustworthy, and closely connected to nature.

Personal Project · YouTube Branding

Nishikigoi with Oliver

This project began when Oliver reached out through a shared Discord server. During our conversations, we discovered a shared appreciation for fish keeping, which led to Oliver telling me about his plans to build a koi pond.

Oliver wanted to document the entire process on YouTube — from construction of the pond to the ongoing care of the fish. He asked me to create the visual identity for the channel.

Freelance · eSports Logo

Rizen

This project was created during my freelance period, when much of my work was focused on the eSports and gaming industry. I specialised in mascot-style logos — illustrated identity marks that combine character design with branding.

The Rizen Phoenix logo was built around the idea of power, rebirth, and resilience. The project allowed me to focus heavily on illustration, expressive shapes, and strong colour contrasts.

Freelance · Brand Identity

Midgard Greenhouse & Nursery

This project was commissioned by a client based in Canada looking to establish a visual identity for their business. Despite being established for several years, the company had never developed a formal corporate identity.

The branding needed to work across staff uniforms, grocery bags, and a flag for the premises — knowing this early helped guide every design decision.
